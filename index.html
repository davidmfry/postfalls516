About the Candidate

Why I'm Running

I am running for Precinct Committeeman to hold the Republican Party accountable to true Christian and conservative values, firmly rooted in the Idaho Constitution — this is the hill I will die on.

In Kootenai County, I will protect our God-given freedoms, strengthen grassroots leadership, and build real community with my neighbors.

I will keep you informed on what's really going on in local government, and demand full transparency with no more backroom deals. The battle for Idaho's future starts right here in our precinct.

"I'm all in — no retreat, no compromise."

Join the Fight

"A precinct committeeman establishes a direct link between the party and the voters in a local electoral precinct — acting as a voice for the people."

— The Role of the Precinct Committeeman

Precinct Committeemen participate in the internal policies of the county party, interact with party leaders and elected officials, and serve as the grassroots foundation of representative government.

Platform

Where I Stand

Clear positions. No spin. Here is exactly what you can expect from David Fry as your Precinct 516 Committeeman.

01

Protecting the Idaho Constitution

The Idaho Constitution is not negotiable. I will fight to ensure every county policy, every party platform, and every elected action aligns with the founding principles of our state — not federal mandates or party establishment pressure.

02

Transparency in Local Government

Backroom deals and closed-door decisions have no place in Kootenai County. I will demand open records, public meetings, and clear communication between government and the citizens it serves.

03

Defending Parental Rights & Education

Parents — not the state, not school boards — are the primary educators of their children. I stand firmly against any curriculum or policy that undermines parental authority or contradicts Christian values in our schools.

04

Second Amendment Rights

The right to keep and bear arms shall not be infringed. Period. Idaho must remain a state where law-abiding citizens can fully exercise their constitutional right to self-defense without government interference.

05

Grassroots Party Accountability

The Republican Party must answer to its voters, not its donors or establishment insiders. As your Precinct Committeeman, I will use every tool at my disposal to hold the party to its principles and its promises.
